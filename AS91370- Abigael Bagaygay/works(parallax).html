A Little Bit About Us

Fragmentum is an organization created recently last 2015 that produces various range of photography which they showcase to inspire and encourage other viewers to engage in photography and to have passion in it. The founder, Ashley Jack, was born and raised in New Zealand. She attended University of Auckland and completed Photography and Media Arts. After her study, she decided to make a website as her portfolio that became popular because of her own definition of aesthetic.
